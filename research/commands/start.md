# Research Start

You are helping a researcher get oriented with the research plugin. Walk through the following steps in order.

## Step 1: Welcome

Display this welcome message:

```
Research Plugin — InfAU

Your AI-powered research assistant for computational design, urban planning,
and sustainable building research. This plugin provides systematic frameworks
for problem selection, project planning, and research strategy.
```

## Step 2: Survey Available Skills

List the research skills available in this plugin:

| Skill | What It Does |
|-------|-------------|
| **Scientific Problem Selection** | Systematic framework for choosing and planning research problems |
| **Deep Research** | Enterprise-grade research with 8-phase pipeline, citation tracking, and multi-source synthesis |

The Scientific Problem Selection framework includes 9 interconnected skills:

1. **Intuition Pumps** — Generate high-quality research ideas
2. **Risk Assessment** — Identify and manage project risks
3. **Optimization Function** — Define success metrics
4. **Parameter Strategy** — Decide what to fix vs. keep flexible
5. **Decision Tree Navigation** — Plan decision points
6. **Adversity Response** — Prepare for crises as opportunities
7. **Problem Inversion** — Navigate around obstacles
8. **Integration & Synthesis** — Synthesize into coherent plan
9. **Meta-Framework** — Orchestrate complete workflow

## Step 3: Research Focus Areas

This plugin is designed for research in:

- **Computational Design**: AI for floor plans, parametric design, generative methods
- **Urban Planning & Mobility**: Sustainable cities, pedestrian simulation, accessibility
- **Participatory Planning**: VR/AR evaluation, citizen feedback, co-design
- **Sustainable Building**: Timber construction, lifecycle assessment, climate-responsive design

## Step 4: Ask How to Help

Ask the researcher what they're working on today. Suggest starting points based on common workflows:

1. **New project planning** — "Help me develop a new research idea"
2. **Troubleshooting** — "I'm stuck on my project and need help navigating"
3. **Risk evaluation** — "Help me assess the risks in my project"
4. **Grant/thesis planning** — "Help me create project documentation"
5. **Strategic decision** — "I need advice on a research strategy question"
6. **Deep research** — "I need a comprehensive analysis of a topic with citations"

Wait for the user's response and guide them to the appropriate skills.

## Entry Points

Based on their response, offer one of three entry points:

**Option 1: Pitch an idea for a new project** — Work it up together
- Begin with Skill 1 (Intuition Pumps)
- Progress through risk assessment and impact evaluation

**Option 2: Share a problem in a current project** — Troubleshoot together
- Begin with Skill 5 (Decision Tree Navigation)
- Use adversity response and problem inversion strategies

**Option 3: Ask a strategic question** — Navigate the decision tree together
- Draw on the most relevant skills for their specific question
- Provide targeted guidance and frameworks
